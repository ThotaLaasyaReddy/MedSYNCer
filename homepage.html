Organ Donation Process

The organ donation process involves several steps to ensure that organs are ethically and efficiently allocated to those in need:

  1. Registration: Individuals register as organ donors through their state registry or when getting a driver's license.
  2. Medical Evaluation: When a potential donor is identified, medical professionals evaluate the viability of organs for donation.
  3. Matching: Available organs are matched with compatible recipients based on medical criteria and urgency.
  4. Recovery: Organs are surgically removed in a sterile operating room by specialized surgeons.
  5. Transportation: Organs are quickly transported to the transplant center where the recipient is waiting.
  6. Transplantation: The recipient undergoes surgery to receive the donated organ.
